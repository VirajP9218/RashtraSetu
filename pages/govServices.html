Digital India

About

Digital India is a flagship program to transform India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy. It focuses on enhancing digital infrastructure, digital literacy, and delivery of government services electronically.

Key Benefits
  • Online access to over 100+ government services
  • Digital payments and reduced cash dependency
  • Improved transparency and accountability
  • Faster service delivery
  • Increased citizen engagement
Objective

Bridge the digital divide between urban and rural areas, enable e-governance, and encourage innovation through IT-enabled services.

Vision

Every citizen digitally empowered with inclusive access to digital services and promotion of entrepreneurship through digital platforms.

Mission

Develop digital infrastructure, provide universal digital literacy, and deliver services digitally through mobile & internet.

Programs
  • DigiLocker, BharatNet, UMANG App, National e-Governance Plan
  • MyGov citizen engagement platform

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

About

The Clean India Mission focuses on improving sanitation, hygiene, and waste management across India. It aims to eliminate open defecation and promote cleanliness awareness.

Key Benefits
  • Improved public health & hygiene
  • Reduction in diseases like diarrhea and cholera
  • Improved aesthetics of urban and rural areas
  • Generation of employment in waste management
Objective

Achieve open-defecation-free (ODF) status nationwide, promote proper solid waste management, and engage communities in cleanliness initiatives.

Vision

A clean, healthy, and sustainable environment with behavioral change in sanitation habits, and public toilets for all.

Mission

Construction of toilets, public awareness campaigns, and citizen-led initiatives like 'Swachh Survekshan'.

Programs
  • Swachh Bharat Gramin (rural) & Swachh Bharat Urban
  • Plastic Waste Management
  • Awareness campaigns in schools, colleges, & workplaces

Ayushman Bharat

About

  • Cashless and paperless hospitalization
  • Free preventive health checkups
  • Access to more than 20,000 hospitals nationwide
Objective

Reduce out-of-pocket healthcare expenditure, ensure equitable access to healthcare, and promote preventive health.

Vision

Healthier population with reduced disease burden and strong primary healthcare infrastructure.

Establish Health & Wellness Centers, integrate preventive & curative healthcare, and facilitate telemedicine & digital health records.

  • Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY)

Skill India

About

Skill India aims to train millions of youth in industry-relevant skills to enhance employability and entrepreneurship capabilities.

Key Benefits
  • Training, skill certification & recognition
  • Apprenticeship and placement support
  • Entrepreneurship & startup mentorship
  • Development of a global workforce
Objective

Create a skilled workforce for industry demands, reduce unemployment, and promote self-employment opportunities.

Vision

India as a global skill hub with every youth empowered with at least one employable skill.

Mission

Skill development programs via NSDC & Ministry of Skill Development, digital learning, and vocational integration.

Programs
  • Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)
  • National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS)
  • Skill Development Centers & ITIs

Startup India

About

Startup India fosters entrepreneurship by providing funding, mentorship, and regulatory support to startups, aiming to make India a global innovation hub.

Key Benefits
  • Tax exemptions & funding support
  • Faster registration & compliance processes
  • Mentorship & incubation support
  • Networking & market access opportunities
Objective

Promote innovation-led entrepreneurship, provide a conducive ecosystem for startups, and support scaling of Indian startups globally.

Vision

India as the world's leading startup ecosystem, creating jobs & economic growth through entrepreneurship.

Mission

Provide funding, incubation, mentorship, simplify compliance, and encourage research & technology adoption.

Programs
  • Startup India Hub (online platform for startups)
  • Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS)
  • Atal Innovation Mission (AIM)
  • Recognition & Incentives for startups
